The Fleet

Check out our Magnificent Six floating palaces!

Neptune

Our prized flagship, mv Neptune is the oldest of Jon's fleet, and currently operates our Baltic Sea Tour. Constructed in 1962 at the Damen shipyard in Rotterdam, she is boasted for her endurance and longevity

Poseidon

Poseidon was Jon's Cruises' first in a new generation of purpose-built superliners. Commissioned for long distance ocean crossings, she operates a fortnightly service between Plymouth, Cork and New York

Titan

The latest edition to Jon's fleet, mv Titan was launched from Aker shipping yard in Helsinki, and delivered to Jon's Cruises in February 1987. This vessel currently operates our celebrated Caribean tour

Oceanus

Heralded as the Queen of the Pacific, mv Oceanus has toured the South Pacific islands for over 20 years, docking at over 30 countries. The ship has stunning features, including a pool, leisure area, and a cocktail bar

Njordor

Touring the Norwegian fjord, mv Njordor has a dintinctly nordic theme, including authentic scandinavian food in its restaurants, IKEA furnishings and Sitka spruce wood pannellings in her atrium

Damona

Damona operates our Mediteranean tours, with stops in locations across Europe, North Afria and the Middle-east. She boasts a high cruising speed combined with space and comfort
